Oregon Concealed Handgun Licensing

This course is provided free to anyone seeking to learn Oregon CHL law. If you would like a certificate for proof of handgun training in order to obtain your CHL, the certificate may be purchased after successful completion of the course for $60.

CONCEALED HANDGUN LICENSE (CHL) SAFETY COURSE

This CHL Online Safety Course has been designed to prepare you for carrying a concealed handgun in the State of Oregon.

  • STEP 1 - TAKE THE COURSE (FREE)
  • STEPS 2 - PURCHASE CERTIFICATE ($60)
  • STEP 3 - APPLY FOR CHL
  • Completion of this course and purchase of the certification can be used for the Oregon Concealed Handgun License Application.
